About Me

Welcome to my page! I am currently a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at the Washington University in St. Louis (WashU). Before joining WashU, I was a graduate student in the Embedded Control Systems and Networking Laboratory(PI: Dr. Jagannathan Sarangapani) at the Missouri University of Science and Technology. In my postdocoral research, I was affiliated with Brain Dynamics and Control Research Group(PI: Dr. ShiNung Ching) and Applied Mathematics Lab(PI: Dr. Jr-Shin Li) at WashU. I am currently hosted by Dr. Jr-Shin Li at WashU.
